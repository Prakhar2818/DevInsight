# DevInsight Minimalist Light Design System

## Overview
A clean, airy, and sophisticated light theme inspired by PitchBot. The UI emphasizes minimalism, rounded typography, soft pastel gradient backgrounds, and elegant 3D card layouts with subtle drop shadows.

## Color Palette
- **Background (Primary)**: Soft white/off-white (`#f8fafc`).
- **Background (Secondary)**: Pure white (`#ffffff`) for elevated cards.
- **Foreground (Primary Text)**: Deep slate (`#0f172a`) for maximum readability.
- **Foreground (Secondary Text)**: Soft slate (`#475569`) for descriptions.
- **Accents**: 
  - Soft Sky Blue (`#38bdf8`)
  - Warm Peach (`#fdba74`)
  - Soft Indigo (`#818cf8`)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Minimalist Sans-serif or JetBrains Mono.
- **Headers**: Bold, tracking-tight, using Deep Slate.
- **Body**: Regular weight, Soft slate color.

## Components

### Cards (3D Minimalist)
- **Background**: Pure white (`#ffffff`).
- **Border**: Very subtle 1px border (`#e2e8f0`).
- **Border Radius**: Large rounded corners (`border-radius: 16px` or `24px`).
- **Shadow**: Soft, diffused drop shadow simulating floating 3D depth (`0 20px 40px -15px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`).
- **Interactions**: On hover, cards subtly float up (`-translate-y-1`) with an increased shadow spread.

### Auth Layout (Split-Screen)
- **Left Pane**: Features a beautiful pastel gradient art piece (mix of soft yellow, peach, and sky blue) or a subtle dotted pattern.
- **Right Pane**: A pure white form container on a light gray background.
- **Inputs**: Light gray background (`#f1f5f9`), rounded borders, solid padding. Focus states use a subtle blue ring.
- **Buttons**: Deep solid black or slate (`#0f172a`) with white text and rounded pill shapes.

### Hero Section
- **Background**: A large, soft radial gradient blending peach, sky blue, and white.
- **Layout**: Center-aligned minimalist typography.
- **Floating UI**: Embedded floating white cards inside the hero demonstrating features.
